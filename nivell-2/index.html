A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite website. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favorite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easy share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
